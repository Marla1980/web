First part: Videographies // Microlecture // 2011
Coordinated by Sala d’Art Jove // Urgell civic centre // Introduced by Eloy Fernández Porta

What’s a referent? Bad Influences reflects on all those inusual and not academical references to the artists. Quotation, appropriation, recycling or plagiarism are currently more porous than ever. In this microlecture, I talk about creative process using referents like Werner Herzog, Bob Esponja, Ian Curtis, Agustín Fernández Mallo or Samuel Beckett.

Second part: Radiophonies

Live radio show // 2011
Coordinated by Sala d’Art Jove // Fabra i Coats Fàbrica de Creació // Introduced by Mery Cuesta

What’s a referent? Bad Influences reflects on all those inusual and not academical references to the artists. Quotation, appropriation, recycling or plagiarism are currently more porous than ever. In this radio show, I rephrase a tale written by Agustín Fernández Mallo that is a remake of La Parábola de Cervantes y el Quijote, a short story written by Jorge Luis Borges.
