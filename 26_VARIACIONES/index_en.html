From a series of anecdotes –real or not– about Johann Sebastian Bach’s life, 26 Variations on the Negligible Possibility reflects on concepts such as “appropiation”, “copy”, “variation” or “plagiarism”, showing fallacy as something unavoidable and human being as somebody predisposed to distort facts.
