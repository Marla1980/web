1972, Iraq. There was a massive poisoning with home-made bread made with wheat flour contaminated with fungicide. The epidemic affected more than 6500 people, there was 459 deaths. Saccharomyces Cerevisiae approaches subjectively to those historical events with a fake interview and a series of photographs of an abandoned bakery, building a fictitious past with real facts.
