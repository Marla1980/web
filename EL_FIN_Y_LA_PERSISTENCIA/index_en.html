(From the series: Annotations to an Eisegesis)

Series: Methacrylate boxes (19,5x15x7,5 cm each), paper pulp blocks, photographic triptychs (25x52 cm each) // 2014

Art Contemporani de la Generalitat Valenciana, Tres anys d’adquisicions, Centre del Carme, Valencia /Las Cigarreras, Alicante, 2021

In The End and the Persistance, the death of the main characters of some novels (Death in Venice, Chronicle of a Death Foretold, Madame Bovary, The Virgin Suicides, Ana Karenina, Lolita, The Stranger, A Brave New World, The Passion According to Renée Vivien) faces persistence of literature. The last sentence of each novel remains intact and the rest is turned into paper pulp blocks that keep the same size as the original novels.
