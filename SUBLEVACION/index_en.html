April Uprising is a project by Petia Cervera Krupova, fictional artist created by Jose Begega and Marla Jacarilla. Petia Cervera Krupova is a young bulgarian postconceptual artist who works on themes such as memory, subjectivity and archive. Influenced by artists such as Alexandr Sokurov, Otolith Group, Ignaci Aballí, Iñaki Bonillas o Harum Farocki among others, Petia imitates common patterns in Barcelona emerging arts, is a blend of all that is welcome by members of the jury and art curators, adapts to the prevailing currents of the context.
Behind this creation process of a fictitious identity there is an intention of transgressing the rules to analize the different agents related to the context. By means of the introduction of this ficticious artist in Sala d’Art Jove, their authors create a device to reflect on the rules and languages that build the emerging art context in Barcelona.
