November of 2011. A catalan filmmaker disappeared after being awarded with four million euros grants from Generalitat de Cataluña. He was accused of falsifying the rasing of his own movies (He directed more than 30 films in less than 10 years) in order to get these grants. In Portrait of a Fictional Artist, I make a list with 99 living artists and get 99 graphs which show their market position. Among them, I insert a graph that shows the market position of this filmmaker.
