Transfers on wood // 13x18 cm y 30x40 cm// Open series // 2017
IEI Award-Embarrat 2018 // Recreando Ruinas, La Escocesa Arts Center, 2018
Esc Out, Desvíos de las prácticas artísticas en la esfera pública, Fabra i Coats Contemporary Arts Center, Barcelona, 2019
2022, Under Destruction, Casal Solleric, Palma de Mallorca, 2020

This series contains images of abandoned and dilapidated factories which are combined with quotes from novels written in different periods of time. Contrary to what spectators may expect, these quotes are fictitious and their authors are not real. The factories, which have been abandoned and are no longer being used, become a different place and, therefore, they are more suitable for reflection than for production. The fictitious quotes are assimilated by the spectators as real quotes and, somehow, they become part of the collective literary imaginary as if they were real.
