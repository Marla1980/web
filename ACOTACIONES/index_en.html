(From the series: Thoughts on the Characteristics the Literature of the Future Should Have, or Not)

Theatrical installation / video
25’53”
2012
Certamen Art Públic, Valencia University
BCN Producció 2013, Espai Cub
Release date: October, 11th, 2012. I Festival Internacional De Teatre Amb Cinc Propostes Teatrals d’Artistes Visuals Amb Mes Aviat Poca Experiencia Teatral, Nauestruch (Sabadell)

Drama in four acts ranging among the tragedy, the comedy, the farce and the metalinguistic. Story in which a false demiurge talks to existing characters belonging to four different plays. Decontextualization and intentional appropriation exercise. Myse en abyme that reflects on the autonomy of the characters and the possible absence of the performers. Theatrical installation in which I dialogue with four characters from different plays (Fando y Lis, Krapp’s Last Tape, Death of a Salesman and Rhinoceros) and take them to the present time. There are no actors playing those characters on stage, I only use the empty stage, spotlights, and words to represent them.
