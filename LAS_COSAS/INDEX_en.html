Digital video // 60’ // 2023 // Barcelona Crea Grant, Agita Grant
Actresses: Lucía Lerendegui and María Gracía Vera. // Voice-over: Eloy Fernández Porta // Assistant cinematographer: Aaron Cabañas // Music and sound editing: Néstor Català

Reinterpretation of the novel Things, a story of the sixties, by Georges Perec, in which the enumerations and descriptions that make up Perec’s text are replaced by conversations between the two protagonists about the consumer society, capitalism and the need for human beings to adapt to a system that constantly excludes them.
