Compilation, organisation and classification –in alphabetical order and by frequency of occurrence– of the 6719 words that make up six of the investiture speeches delivered by the different prime ministers of Spain since 1976 until 2011. (Adolfo Suárez, Leopoldo Calvo-Sotelo, Felipe González, José María Aznar, José Luis Rodríguez Zapatero and Mariano Rajoy).
