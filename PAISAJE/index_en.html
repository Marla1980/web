Landscape

Digital video
22'
2020
Direction, script, editing and voice-over: Marla Jacarilla
Sound editing, music and voiceover: Néstor Català

The landscape surrounds us, shapes us and conditions us. We inhabit it, but it also inhabits us. It transforms itself all the time and transforms us with it, for better or for worse. In exceptional circumstances, it becomes exceptional. It changes all the time because it is, after all, a living organism.

*This work has been carried out during the state of alarm declared by the Spanish Government in 2020 due to the pandemic caused by the coronavirus. To make it, materials compiled by the author and recorded at different times and places without a specific purpose have been used.
