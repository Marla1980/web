Heroic Uselessness,Useless Heroism

(From the series: Annotations to an Eisegesis)

Diptych on wood
50x70 cms
2016
Héroes y antiheroes, Madrid, 2016

According to the dictionary of the Royal Academy of the Spanish language, “something practical is something that brings utility or produces immediate material compensation”. Do the artists have impractical behavior? Do the heroes have to be necessarily practical? Could an artist become a hero? Does it make sense for him to try it?
In this work, a series of graphics showing the different GDP of many countries are covered by a Ionesco quote in gold letters: S’il faut absolument que l’art ou le théâtre serve à quelque chose je dirai qu’il devrait servir à apprendre aux gens qu’il y a des activités qui ne servent à rien, et qu’il est indispensable qu’il y en ait.
