(From the series: Annotations to an Eisegesis)

Single-channel video
Color
20’
La Nit dels Museus, Arxiu Fotogràfic de Barcelona, 2017

The world and everything else suggests a reinterpretation of some of the images shown in the book of the multidisciplinary artist Oleguer Junyent, Ròda’l món and tórna al Born, mixing them with autobiographical elements and images from different sources. A subjective reflection on travelling as a self-knowledge and exorcism method.
