(From the series: Annotations to an Eisegesis)

Installation: 38 handmade books
Dimensions variable
2011
EAC, Encuentros de arte contemporáneo, MUA (Alicante University Museum), 2013 // L’escriptura en el relat artístic, Palau Foundation, Caldes d’Estrac, 2022

Work based on an article written by Enrique Vila-Matas about the first sentences of the novels. In this work, I make an inventory of all the books in my room and use the first sentences written on them to create 38 different beginnings for 38 different novels.
