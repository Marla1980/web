Installation: 10 framed photographs, 82x44 cm. 707 letters and orthographic signs that make up the story by Jorge Luis Borges, Del rigor en la ciencia, made in Epoxy resin, methacrylate box // 2021

The writer Jorge Luis Borges reflects in his story, Del rigor en la ciencia, on the inevitable uselessness of maps that are too accurate and detailed, thus emphasizing our impossibility of copying reality without interpreting it.
