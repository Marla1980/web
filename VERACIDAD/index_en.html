(From the series: Thoughts on the Characteristics the Literature of the Future Should Have, or Not)

Single-channel video // 5’10’’ // Colour // Sound
Valls Biennial // 2013

The Improbable Veracity of the History I Am Telling questions history departing from the childhood of the polish writer Witold Gombrowicz to end up combining it with my own childhood. By showing the mechanism of constructing fiction, The improbable veracity of the History I Am Telling directly interrogates the viewer and shows the way history is constructed.
