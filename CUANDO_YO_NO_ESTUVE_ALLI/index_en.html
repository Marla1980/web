(From the series: Annotations to an Eisegesis)

Performance
30’
Nit dels Museus, Photographic Archives of Barcelona
2016

When I Was Not There proposes a subjective review of some images part of the exhibition Pérez de Rozas. La crónica gràfica de Barcelona 1931-1954, and reflects on the many aims, intentions and motivations of the photographic fact. Starting from some images part of  this exhibition, this performance interprets them by blending historical data with autobiographical or even fictional.  

Collaborative project between Photographic Archives of Barcelona and La Escocesa Arts Centre
