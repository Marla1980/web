Installation in collaboration with Enrique Baeza
294 DIN A3 prints, methacrylate urn, sound installation
Esther Montoriol Gallery, Barcelona
2020

Denouement, the first joint exhibition by Enrique Baeza and Marla Jacarilla, takes as a starting point the story of tragic heroes and opens a door to redemption. The project shows a room full of names of tragic characters who have been unable to escape a fatal fate. At the back of the room a transparent urn invites visitors to participate in a sound installation where many people tell about their tragic destiny and how are they escaping to it.
