Publicacions de la Brossa
2021

This book is the result of a performance carried out between January 10 and 31, 2021, in the framework of the exhibition Postbrossa2020. In it, the visitor sent by e-mail a word or short phrase to the author and, from that word, she created a fictitious memory and wrote it by hand on a sheet of paper, scanned it and sent it by e-mail to each person. This publication compiles the 39 memories written during those days.
