Single-channel video //5’ // Colour // No sound // Subtitles // 2011
La Condición Narrativa, La Capella, Barcelona, 2012
Art Souterrain Festival, Montreal, 2013


First part of the series inspired by George’s Perec Species of Spaces. From fragments of this essay, I develope a reinterpretation of an abstract space and its components. The shot corresponds to the ground of a courtyard ―a hand held camera travelling― which, in its abstraction, is compared to a land area map.
