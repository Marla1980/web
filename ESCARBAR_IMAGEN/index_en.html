Analogue photographs, digital manipulated photographs, digital illustrations, texts

Series

From some analogue black and white photographs showing people who are completely unknown for me, I reflect on them, reinterpret, manipulate and distort them to find some clues that allow me to analyse in detail our relationship with images and how they have evolved since the advent of the digital era.
