Performance 
Fundació Antoni Tàpies
50' // 2022 

This performance is part of the cycle of activities around the exhibition Goshka Macuga. In Movement.

With the collaboration of Andrés Duque, Juan David Galindo and Mado Serra.

Hybrid, experimental epistolary exchange, structured in six chapters, which takes a subjective look at some of the ways in which censorship has been applied over the years in areas such as war correspondence, cinema, literature, visual arts and the configuration of space through Google Maps.
