Installation // typewriters, texts, framed photos of various sizes, negatoscope image // 2022 // After, La Escocesa, Loop video art festival.

Installation that reflects on the passage of time, the concept of obsolescence and the act of writing. Childhood images of various writers give rise to a lucubration about their motivations for dedicating their time to writing, challenging with this attitude the accelerationist dynamics that prevent us from taking the necessary time to understand everything that is happening around us.
