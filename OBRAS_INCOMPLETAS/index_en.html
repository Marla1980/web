(From the series: Annotations to an Eisegesis)

Installation: 10 framed photographs (10 x 15 cm), 10 audios // 2014
As a part of the cycle Relaciones ortográficas (en tiempos revueltos), Espaidos, Sala Muncunill, Terrassa, 2014

Choice and manipulation of ten famous paintings of art history. Removal of the main characters and reinterpretation of the empty space by means of ten short stories structured from ten different rhetorical figures (ellipsis, personification, prosopography, paronomasia, enumeration, animalization, éthopée, concatenation, captatio benevolentiae and proactive narration).
