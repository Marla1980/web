Installation: 101 pictures, 301 sheets of paper, book
Dimensions variable
2010
Any similarity with reality is pure coincidence, Sala d’ Art Jove, Barcelona, 2011
L'Espai Invisible, Piramidón Contemporary Arts Center, 2016
With the support of Piramidón Contemporary Arts Center

For five months, the artist travels across all the subway stations in Barcelona, trying to find 101 people to write about. She takes pictures of them and writes ficcional stories. Before finding all this people, writes in a notebook everything that happens while waiting.
