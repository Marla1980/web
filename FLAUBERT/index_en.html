Artist’s book (Ed 3) // 15x22 cm // 92 pages // 2011
Group exhibition Mientras quepa en la maleta: LAKKA studioart (México DF), No Automático (Monterrey), Museo del Ámbar (San Cristóbal de las Casas).

“Bureaucracy”, “frontier”, “distance”. These three words and their meanings divide the book into three chapters, based on the unfortunate facts that, unexpectedly, became a series of exhibitions in three different cities of Mexico. The story has two different levels: the first one, in wich there is a linear story; and the second one, in wich we can find the meanings of the three keywords.
