(From the series: Thoughts on the Characteristics the Literature of the Future Should Have, or Not)

Installation: single-channel video, Six False Novels autographed copy, twenty two photographs (13x18 cms each), report of a handwriting expert
2015
B/W and colour
85 min aprox
BBVA Foundation, Video Art Grant // Valls Biennial 2017

According to Manuel Vicent, instruction manual is the literature of the future. According to G.K. Chesterton, absurdist fiction is the literature of the future. Taking these seemingly unrelated and contradictory keys as a starting point, in Instruction Manual for the Understanding of False Novels, I make up an ironic manual, an instructive video to understanding false novels by using the Six False Novels written by Ramón Gómez de la Serna.
