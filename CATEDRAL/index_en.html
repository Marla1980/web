(From the series: Thoughts on the Characteristics the Literature of the Future Should Have, or Not)

Installation: handled Raymond Carver’s story book Beginners, video
42’11”
Dimensions variable
2013
As a part of the cycle Catedrals a la Capella, Capella de Sant Roc, Valls

Taking the Raymond Carver’s story Cathedral as a starting point, I interpret it (in a completely subjective way) as autobiographical, comparing the main characters with Carver himself and his editor, Gordon Lish, who cut up some of the Carver stories to 75%, creating a myth that actually never existed; the minimalist Carver we all know.
