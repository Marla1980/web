Elements for a Landscape

 

Installation // 4 canvases 81 x 65 cm, 3 canvases 100 x 73cm, 1 canvas 100 x 65 cm, 100 text notes 10.5 x 9.5 cm Fuga Gallery, Barcelona

What if people were also landscape? Human bodies as mountains, as rivers, as valleys and plains. Human bodies that change, grow and evolve, that transform. Human bodies that, like those oceans, mountains, and valleys, are constantly being constructed, deconstructed and reconstructed, forming a fundamental part of our artistic imaginary since time immemorial. Elements for a Landscape proposes a subjective journey through some aspects that could form part of a possible landscape. Images and texts that provide feedback by combining in different ways, thus creating an atlas that is as unfinished as it is open to multiple readings.
